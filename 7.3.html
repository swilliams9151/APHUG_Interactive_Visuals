3. Gender Inequality Index (GII)

GII measures the loss of achievement due to gender inequality.
Lower Score = Better (0.0 is perfect equality).

3 Components (SPS-7.C.2)

  • Reproductive Health: Maternal mortality & Teen birth rate.
  • Empowerment: Seats in legislature & Secondary education.
  • Labor Market: Female labor force participation rate.
