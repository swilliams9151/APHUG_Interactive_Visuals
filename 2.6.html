Positive Checks

Increases Death Rate

Events that shorten life span to reduce the population. Malthus saw these as "natural" outcomes of overpopulation.
Examples: Famine, War, Disease, Disaster.

Preventive Checks

Decreases Birth Rate

Voluntary actions taken by people to limit population growth before a crisis occurs.
